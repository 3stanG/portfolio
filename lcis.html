LOCATIONAL CLEARANCE INFORMATION SYSTEM

Is a web application that offers an information system designed to enhance efficiency, accessibility, and transparency in facilitating locational clearance transactions at MPDC.

FEATURES

LCIS, a collaborative effort between myself and my former college classmate, encompasses features that I managed during our project.

DASHBOARD

The dashboard exhibits real-time data on the overall number of applicants per barangay, the classification totals for LC projects in GMA Cavite, and the classification totals per barangay.

SCANNER

The scanner meticulously tracks application progress, offering comprehensive insights into their current status, ensuring transparency and efficiency by accurately pinpointing their whereabouts within the workflow.

EMPLOYEE

This module handles employee management, including addition, removal, and access level settings. It also facilitates updating employee information and collecting signatures and personal other details.

EMPLOYEE ARCHIVES

The Employee Archives in the module displays both active and removed employees, allowing reactivation. Only super admins have access, ensuring efficient employee record management.

PROFILE

In the profile section, individuals can update specific details such as their name, profile picture, password, and title. This restricted access ensures that users can only modify essential personal information.
